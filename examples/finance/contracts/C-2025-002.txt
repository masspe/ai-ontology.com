Logistics Integration Agreement — Contract C-2025-002

Effective date: April 1, 2025
Term: 24 months, fixed.

Parties:
  - Globex (the "Integrator"), Wilmington, Delaware, USA.
  - Initech (the "Customer"), Austin, Texas, USA.

Scope:
  Globex will provide an end-to-end logistics integration for Initech's
  enterprise software supply chain. Work includes a custom REST connector
  between Globex's TMS and Initech's procurement platform, three days of
  on-site training in Austin, and a Q1 retainer covering planning and
  discovery.

Commercial terms:
  Total contract value: 120,000 EUR for the first 12 months. Billed
  quarterly. Net 45 payment terms. Renewal pricing to be negotiated 60
  days before expiry.

Signatories:
  Bob Chen, VP Operations — Globex
  Carol Diaz, Head of Procurement — Initech
