Software Licensing Agreement — Contract C-2025-003

Effective date: July 1, 2025
Term: 12 months.

Parties:
  - Acme Labs (the "Licensor"), Lyon, France.
  - Initech (the "Licensee"), Austin, Texas, USA.

Scope:
  Acme Labs grants Initech a non-exclusive, non-transferable annual
  licence to deploy Acme's industrial automation control software ("Tier
  A" edition) on up to fifty Initech-owned production lines. The licence
  bundles premium support: 24/7 incident response, two scheduled review
  meetings per quarter, and prioritized hotfix delivery.

Commercial terms:
  Total contract value: 80,000 EUR for the year. Billed in two parts:
  45,000 EUR upfront for the licence and 35,000 EUR for premium support
  in September. Net 30 payment terms.

Signatories:
  Alice Martin, CTO — Acme Labs
  Carol Diaz, Head of Procurement — Initech
