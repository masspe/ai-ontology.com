Master Services Agreement — Contract C-2025-001

Effective date: January 15, 2025
Term: 12 months, with automatic renewal for additional 12-month periods
unless either party terminates with 90 days written notice.

Parties:
  - Acme Labs (the "Provider"), registered in Lyon, France.
  - Globex (the "Client"), registered in Wilmington, Delaware, USA.

Scope:
  Acme Labs will design, build, and integrate a sensor-based industrial
  automation pilot at the Globex Memphis distribution hub. The pilot
  covers the receiving dock and one outbound sortation lane. Deliverables
  include hardware install, firmware, an API for the Globex WMS, and
  on-site training for two operators.

Commercial terms:
  Total contract value: 50,000 EUR. Billed in two milestone invoices of
  18,000 EUR (Q1) and 32,000 EUR (Q2). Net 30 payment terms.

Signatories:
  Alice Martin, CTO — Acme Labs
  Bob Chen, VP Operations — Globex
